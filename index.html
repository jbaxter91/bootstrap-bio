About Me

I am a family man, my kids come first. I can be serious, but my default is to live and have fun. I have my BCS from Western Governors University. I love to program! I am not artistic at all so programming fills my need to create. I think that with computers and programming that nothing is impossible and i love to tackle problems others say cannot be done.

In 2016 I co-founded a company with my cousin called Eridian Entertainment. In three months, we released our first game to the google play store called "Lil Fish Go". In an attempt to lower the file size, I ended up messing up the code beyond repair and realized I had no backup. From that moment on I swore to do everything in my power to not only backup regularly, but also use VCS. Lil Fish still is one of the most painful lessons I have learned but I am happy it happened because it made me a better developer.
